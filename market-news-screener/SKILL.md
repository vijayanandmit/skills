# Market News Screener

## Purpose

Filter current market news into actionable catalysts and risks. Prioritize what can move prices, earnings expectations, valuation, or sentiment.

## Required Research

Always use current sources. Check reliable financial news, company press releases, filings, economic calendars, central bank updates, sector data, and current price and volume.

For each catalyst, verify:

1. Source and timestamp
2. Whether the information is new or already expected
3. Affected tickers and peer read-through
4. Price reaction and volume confirmation
5. Expected duration: intraday, swing trade, or long-term thesis
6. Counterarguments and upcoming events

## Catalyst Classification

Use one of:

- Earnings
- Guidance
- M&A
- Regulation
- Product / customer win
- Macro data
- Rates / currency
- Commodity move
- Analyst action
- Legal or fraud risk
- Social hype
- No material catalyst

## Output Format

Use this structure:

## Executive Summary

Summarize the highest-impact news and market tone.

## Catalyst Table

| Ticker / Asset | News | Catalyst Type | Price Reaction | Tradeability | Risk | Takeaway |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

## Best Opportunities

Rank the top 3 tradeable setups with entry trigger, target, stop-loss, and time horizon.

## Avoid / Fade List

Flag crowded, stale, or low-quality headlines.

## What To Watch Next

List upcoming events that can confirm or invalidate the moves.

## Compliance

Do not present trades as guaranteed. Treat the output as market decision support.
